Who We Are?

We are a dedicated group of students from EMLyon Business School and École Centrale Lyon, united by a profound passion for environmental conservation. Our diverse team comprises individuals who bring unique perspectives and expertise to the table: Deyi, Leyan, Mellissa, Haofei, and Yunze. Together, we share a common vision of a world where sustainability is not a choice, but a way of life.

Our Mission

At Midori Movement, our mission is crystal clear: to achieve global carbon neutrality by the year 2050. We firmly believe that this ambitious goal is the cornerstone for enabling long-term sustainable development for every individual on this planet. Through unwavering commitment and collaborative efforts, we aspire to create a legacy of environmental stewardship that will resonate for generations to come.
